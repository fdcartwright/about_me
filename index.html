About Me - The story of Dan Cartwright

With my base knowledge in web development and the skills I will obtain from CodeFellows, my goal is land a .JS development contract and build a diverse experience coding languages. The goal is to provide me the experience required to land a full-time permanent position with a large Seattle based or remote tech company.
